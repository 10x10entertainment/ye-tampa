If your bid is not selected, you are not charged anything. No payment is collected until you are contacted as a winner and complete the Stripe checkout. There is no risk in submitting a bid.
How will tickets be transferred to me? +
Tickets are transferred digitally upon payment confirmation. You will receive transfer instructions directly from 10x10 Entertainment via the contact info you provide at checkout.

Full afterparty details — including venue, time, and entry instructions — are sent directly to confirmed buyers. You will have everything you need well before showtime.
